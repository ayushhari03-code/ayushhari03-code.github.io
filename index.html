About Me

I'm a passionate MSc Computer Science student with a strong foundation in software development and data analytics. I graduated with a BCA degree (CGPA 7.7) and am currently pursuing my postgraduate studies at Sahodaran Ayyappan Smaraka SNDP Yogam College, Konni.
